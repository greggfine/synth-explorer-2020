Explore our unique collection of vintage analog synthesizers

Authentic Analog

True analog synthesis allows you to quickly and easily whip up virtually any monophonic — or polyphonic — sound you can dream up. Each synth in our collection has authentic VCO, VCF, VCA, and ladder filter designs working in concert with fully analog triangle/square wave VCOs.

Classic Circuits

Our classic circuits consist of matched transistors and JFETs, ultra-high-precision 0.1% thin film resistors, and polyphenylene sulfide capacitors. In addition, each synth in the collection includes stereo analog Chorus, and true analog Distortion (overdrive) circuits.
